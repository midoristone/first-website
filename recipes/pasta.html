Bowtie Pasta

Ingredients

  1. Bowtie pasta
  2. Rao's red sauce
  3. Vegan feta cheese (Trader Joe's or Violife recommended)

Optional Ingredients

  • Additional herbs such as basil
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe steps

  1. Take the desired amount of the vegan feta block and chop into small pieces.
  2. Bring water to a boil in a large pot.
  3. Add the bowtie pasta to the boiling water and cook to desired consistency.
  4. While the bowtie pasta cooks, add your red sauce to a small saucepan. Add your chopped vegan feta and bring to a boil over low heat. Stir regularly to prevent the pot from burning!
  5. When the pasta is ready, drain the water.
  6. Check your sauce and see if the vegan feta is completely melted. Once it is, turn off the heat and remove the saucepan from the stove.
  7. Portion your pasta and red sauce, and add any garnish you desire.
  8. Enjoy!
