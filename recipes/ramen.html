Instant Ramen

Description

Incredients

  1. Instant ramen noodles, like like these ones here.
  2. Water

Optional ingredients

  • Vegan egg, like Just Egg.
  • Chili sauce
  • Sriracha
  • Lime
  • Green onions

Recipe steps

  1. Bring your water to boil in a small saucepan.
  2. Add the broth powder packet to the boiling water.
  3. Once the broth dissolves into the water, add your noodles. Boil noodles for about 2 minutes.
  4. Once noodles have softened, take saucepan off heat and pour the noodles and broth into a bowl.
  5. Add any additional items. If adding vegan egg, cook the egg separately in a pan and add them to the soup bowl.
  6. Enjoy!
